About GreenLeaf Bistro
-----------------------
GreenLeaf Bistro is a health-focused café and bistro chain serving fresh, locally sourced meals and beverages across 12 locations in the greater metro area. Founded in 2018, we operate seven days a week and pride ourselves on fast, friendly service and sustainable practices. Our mission is to make wholesome food accessible, enjoyable, and affordable for every customer. We serve approximately 4,000 guests per day across all locations, handling over 1.4 million visits annually.

Q: What are your most popular menu items?
A: Our top sellers are the Avocado Toast, Garden Bowl, and Cold Brew Coffee. The Avocado Toast is served on sourdough with cherry tomatoes, microgreens, and a drizzle of chili oil. The Garden Bowl features quinoa, roasted seasonal vegetables, chickpeas, and tahini dressing. Our Cold Brew Coffee is slow-steeped for 20 hours in original, vanilla, and oat-milk variants. Seasonal specials rotate monthly — currently featuring the Mango Chili Wrap, Matcha Chia Pudding, and Roasted Beet Flatbread.

Q: What are your opening hours?
A: We are open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Our kitchen closes 30 minutes before closing time. Three locations (Market Street, Westside, Airport Terminal B) operate extended hours until 10:30 PM on weekdays. Holiday hours are posted on our website and app at least 72 hours in advance.

Q: What is your average wait time?
A: We target under 10 minutes for counter orders during off-peak hours. Peak hours (12:00–1:30 PM and 6:00–8:00 PM) may see 15–25 minute waits. Mobile and online pre-orders are given priority and are typically ready within 8 minutes even during peak times. Wait time data is available on the app, updated every 2 minutes. We deploy additional staff when wait times exceed 15 minutes.

Q: Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
A: Yes. Our menu clearly marks vegan (V), gluten-free (GF), nut-free (NF), dairy-free (DF), and low-FODMAP (LF) options. We maintain separate preparation surfaces and colour-coded utensils for allergen-sensitive orders. Our allergen matrix is available at every counter and on our website. We introduced a dedicated gluten-free prep station at 8 of our 12 locations in March 2026.

Q: How do you handle complaints?
A: All complaints are escalated to the shift manager within 15 minutes of being raised. Refunds or order replacements are offered on the spot for quality issues. For digital complaints (app, web form, or survey), our team responds within 24 hours on weekdays and 48 hours on weekends. Recurring complaints trigger a Tier 3 root-cause review within 72 hours. Every complaint is logged with a reference number and resolution is communicated via the same channel used to submit it.

Q: What is your CSAT target and how do you track it?
A: We aim for a CSAT score of 4.5 out of 5. Scores below 4.0 trigger a root-cause review within 48 hours. CSAT is tracked weekly by location, channel (mobile, web, kiosk, email), and time of day. We break CSAT down by theme (food quality, wait time, staff, cleanliness, pricing, app) to pinpoint which operational area is driving dissatisfaction. Monthly CSAT reports are shared with all team leads. Our internal stretch target is 70% for Q3 2026.

Q: What caused the CSAT decline from March to May 2026?
A: CSAT dropped from 68.5% in March to 47.9% in April and 31.3% in May 2026. Root-cause analysis identified three primary drivers: (1) App-related complaints surged from 24.1% to 52.3% of all complaints following a payment gateway migration that introduced checkout errors on Android. (2) Wait time complaints grew as foot traffic exceeded capacity at two locations. (3) A 4.5% price adjustment in April increased pricing complaints. Our engineering team has deployed hotfixes reducing Android payment failures from 12% to 1.8%. We have also hired four additional part-time staff at the two affected locations.

Q: How are app issues being addressed?
A: The GreenLeaf app experienced stability issues in April-May 2026 following the Order v3.0 platform upgrade: Android payment gateway timeouts (~12% of checkouts), session timeout errors causing lost carts, and push notification delays. Three hotfix releases (v3.0.1–v3.0.3) addressed critical payment flow issues. As of May 31, the payment timeout rate is 1.8%, targeting sub-1% with the v3.1 release in June 2026. Users should update to v3.0.3 or later or use our mobile web at order.greenleafbistro.com as a temporary alternative.

Q: What is your approach to sustainability?
A: 73% of our fresh produce is locally sourced within 100 miles. All packaging is compostable or recyclable; we eliminated single-use plastics in 2023. We donate unsold food daily via SecondHarvest Metro — 47,000 meals donated in 2025. All cafés run on 100% renewable energy. 10-cent discount for customers who bring a reusable cup. Our 2026 goals: 85% local sourcing and zero food waste at 4 pilot locations.

Quarterly refresher training is mandatory. Monthly "Customer First" workshops focus on complaint handling and de-escalation. District managers conduct unannounced quality audits twice per quarter. We invest approximately $1,200 per employee per year in training — above the industry average of $740. Average staff tenure is 2.8 years vs. the 1.1-year QSR industry average.

Disruptions affecting more than 25% of locations for more than 3 days are publicly disclosed on our website.

Q: How do you measure and improve food quality?
A: Our three-part QA framework: (1) Supply QA — every delivery inspected against quality spec sheets; (2) Prep QA — recipe cards with exact weights and temperatures; spot-plate checks every 2 hours; (3) Customer feedback loop — food quality ratings feed into a weekly review; locations below 60% food quality CSAT for two consecutive weeks trigger an in-person audit. In Q1 2026, food quality CSAT was 75.2%, our highest-rated theme.

Q: What measures are in place during illness outbreaks?

We work with a certified occupational health consultant. COVID-era protocols can be reactivated within 24 hours if needed. Food safety always takes priority over throughput.

Q: What is the GreenLeaf app's order tracking feature?

Q: What happens if I accidentally place a duplicate order?

Once in preparation, we can only offer a loyalty wallet credit for the duplicate (not a card refund). To prevent accidental duplicates, our app shows a confirmation screen with a 5-second wait before finalising any order. For recurring accidental duplicates (e.g., due to slow network), updating the app to the latest version typically resolves the issue.

Q: What is your complaint escalation timeline?

Tier 4 (executive team): for legal, media, or reputational complaints. Target resolution at each tier: Tier 1 < 5 min, Tier 2 < 2 hours, Tier 3 < 48 hours, Tier 4 < 72 hours.
